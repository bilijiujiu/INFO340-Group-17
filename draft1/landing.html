1. Search and Save

Find job opportunities that match your goals and save them to your board.

2. Track Progress

Why JobTrack matters

Job hunting information is often scattered across LinkedIn, Handshake, email, calendars, and personal notes. JobTrack brings those pieces together so users can feel more organized and in control.
